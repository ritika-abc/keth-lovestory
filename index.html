Chapter 1: Hariyali aur Akeli Keth

Ye khani hai us time ki, jab hariyali rahti thi, rivers saaf thi aur pachjar ka mosam khile kile ful se bhara tha. Nadi ke kinare ek ladki rehti thi, jiska naam Keth tha. Keth jaada baat karna pasand nahi karti thi, isi liye uska koi dost bhi nahi tha. Vo sara samay apne aap ke saath hi bitati thi.

Keth ke real friends the  vhan ke paltu jaanwar. Vo unki bhasha samaj sakti thi, par pehle aisi nahi thi. Uske kinare ek aur ghar tha jahan uski best friend Kaju rehti thi. Vo dono hamesha sath rehti thi.

Lekin ek din inki dosti aisi tuti ki Keth ki life me pehli baar kisi ke liye itna broken feel hua. Reason ye tha ki Keth bohot khubsurat thi, aur usse pasand karne wale me se ek ladka Sam tha. Kaju ko Sam pasand tha, isliye Kaju ne Keth se dosti tod di. Keth to jaanti bhi nahi thi ki aisa kyun ho raha hai. Keth introvert thi aur logo se jaldi ghulne me dikat hoti thi.

Chapter 2: Jungle aur Shikar

Ab Keth badi ho gayi thi aur jungle me rehkar shikar karti thi. Ek din Keth ki nazar ek rabbit par padi. Vo achanak uske samne aaya aur bola:
“Bchaooo! Bchaooo!”

Keth ne usse baat ki aur pucha, “Kya hua, kyun bhag rahe ho?”
Rabbit ne bola, “Aree vo insaan muje mar hi dega, kitna bura hai.”

“Wait, tum meri bhasha kaise bol pa rahi ho? Tum to khud hi insaan ho!” Keth ne kaha.
“Han, insaan hun, lekin pyar, dard aur ghusse ki bhasha samaj pati hun,” Keth ne jawab diya.

Chapter 3: Shararti Geho aur Hero Rine

Tabhi ek ladka aaya, naam Geho tha. Vo bda shararti ladka tha. Usne rabbit ko pakad rakha tha aur bola,
“Hey ladki, leave it, it’s my rabbit.”

Keth boli, “Oo really, thank it,” lekin Geho bat nahi man rha tha  or Uske dosto ne Keth ko gher liya.

Tabhi is khani ka hero aaya, naam Rine tha. Usne kaha, “Dark eye girl, don’t worry, I’m here.” Vo hamesha dusron ki madat ke liye ready rehta tha. Jolly nature tha, kafeboy jaise dikhta tha, big round hat, black shoes, black eye mask aur haath me laser rope. usne Geho ko rope se pad par latka diya, lekin fit chor diya, kyunki vo bohot achha tha.

Rine ka behavior, mind aur action prince charming jaise tha. Lekin 7 baje hone ki wajah se usse ghar jaana tha. Keth thanks bhi nahi bol paayi aur jaldi vhan se chali gayi, lekin usne apna hair pin kho diya, jo Rine ne le liya.

Chapter 4: Library aur Naye Dost

Next day, Keth us ladke ke baare me sochti rahi. Usse sirf naam pata tha, aur ye bhi nahi pata tha ki vo kaise dikhta hai, kyunki vo eye mask aur round hat pehn ke aaya tha. Isliye Keth ne usse "the mask man" bula diya.

Pure gaav me is insan ki charcha thi. Vo bohot popular tha, lekin koi nahi jaanta tha ki vo kyun logo ki madat karta hai aur kahan rahta hai, kaise dikhta hai.

Keth ab library me job karne lagi thi. Pehli baar usne logo se baat ki aur naye dost bhi banaye. Lekin vo chup chap apna kaam karti rahi, isliye log use akdu samajhne lage. Keth jaanti thi ki sab chale jaate hai, badal jaate hai.

Library me ek ladka Ben aksar book padhne aata tha aur bohot jokes sunata tha. Keth ne himmat karke Ben se baat ki, aur ab vo kaafi ache dost ban gaye. Keth ne phir se dosti par trust kiya aur library me uske ache dost ban gaye.

Chapter 5: Party aur Broken Heart

1 January ko king ne ek rajha jeet liya, aur khushi me party rakhi. Party me vo hi aa sakta tha jisko English aati thi. Keth ko English nahi aati thi, isliye Ben ne uski madat ki.

Keth ko Ben pasand tha, aur usne socha tha ki apna feeling usse bata degi, lekin himmat nahi thi.

Rajmela me jane ke liye Keth ne white gown pehena. Vo bohot pyari lag rahi thi. Uski nazar ek bhed me thi, lekin vo wahi insan dhund rahi thi. 11:40 baje tak Keth soch rahi thi ki 12 baje Ben ko sab bata degi, lekin tabhi usne dekha ki Ben kisi dusri ladki ke sath tha, kafi khush. Keth ne decide kiya ki kuch nahi batayegi. Vo totally broken thi aur andhere me ek nadi me gir  gayi, jo mehel ke andar thi.

Vahan kewal shahi log ja sakte the. Uske bal pure chehre par aa gaye aur dress bhi bhig gayi. Crying aur nose bhi beh rahi thi.

Chapter 6: Rine ki Madat aur Sam ka Sach

Tabhi usne kisi ki awaz suni:
“Dark eye girl, don’t worry, I’m here.”

Rine ne apni pocket se pin nikala aur Keth ke balon me laga diya, aur bola,
“I’m searching you, but I feel you are not okay.”

Keth boli, “Han, vo thik nahi hai aur mujhe kisi ki help nahi chahiye.”

Itna bol kar vo wahan se ja hi rahi thi, tabhi Keth ko pata chala ki ye eye bahut similar thi… aur usne samjha ki wahi Sam hai. sam hi Rine hai same vahi ladka tha jo bachpan me use pasand karta tha.....
